Average Time On Shelter
###
Statistics
###
Any
###
Shows the average time spent on shelter for all animals by year.
###
en
###
SELECT
YEAR(DateBroughtIn) AS biyear,
AVG(DaysOnShelter) AS avtimeonshelter
FROM animal
WHERE DaysOnShelter > 0
GROUP BY YEAR(DateBroughtIn)
