Gift Aid Claim
###
Financials
###
2707/Any
###
Produces a list of all donations qualifying for gift aid between two dates.
###
en_GB
###
$VAR from DATE Enter from date$
$VAR to DATE Enter to date$

SELECT OwnerName, OwnerAddress, OwnerTown, OwnerCounty, OwnerPostcode, HomeTelephone, Donation, ownerdonation.Date
FROM ownerdonation
INNER JOIN owner ON ownerdonation.OwnerID = owner.ID
WHERE ownerdonation.IsGiftAid = 1 AND
ownerdonation.Date >= '$@from$' AND ownerdonation.Date <= '$@to$'
