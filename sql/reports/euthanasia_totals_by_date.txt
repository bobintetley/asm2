Euthanasia Totals by Date
###
Movement Graphs
###
Any
###
Produces a graph of total animals euthanised for each reason between two dates.
###
en
###
$VAR from DATE Enter from date$
$VAR to DATE Enter to date$

SELECT ReasonName, COUNT(animal.ID) AS euthanised
FROM animal
INNER JOIN deathreason ON deathreason.ID = animal.PTSReasonID
WHERE DiedOffShelter = 0 AND IsDOA = 0 AND PutToSleep = 1 AND DeceasedDate >= '$@from$' AND DeceasedDate <= '$@to$'
GROUP BY ReasonName
